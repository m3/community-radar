Council Perspectives

Developer

Focuses on technical feedback, bug reports, and API usage. Uses Radar to spot emerging technical issues before they become support tickets.

Community Manager

Tracks overall sentiment, top voices, and engagement levels. Uses Radar to measure the impact of recent announcements or events.

Studio Lead

Monitors brand health, competitor movement, and high-level trends. Uses Radar to make strategic resource allocation decisions.

The Metric Lab

Sentiment Ratio

A balanced view of positive versus negative mentions. Neutral comments are excluded to highlight polarizing topics.

Ratio = (Positive) / (Positive + Negative) * 100

Engagement Score

Measures the virality and interaction depth of content, weighing comments heavier than simple upvotes.

Score = (Upvotes * 1) + (Comments * 5) + (Shares * 10)

Brand Blips

Sudden spikes in mentions or sentiment shifts. Blips trigger alerts when velocity exceeds 3 standard deviations from the 7-day rolling average, acting as an early warning system for PR crises or viral successes.
